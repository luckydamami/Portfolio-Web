About Me

Hello! My name is Lucky, and I am an aspiring web developer with a strong passion for creating interactive and user-friendly web experiences. I hail from Bandanwara, a small village in Ajmer, Rajasthan. My journey into web development began with a deep interest in technology and a certified course in programming languages, where I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I later expanded my skills to include React.js, MySQL, and Git/GitHub through self-learning. With a non-technical academic background in B.Sc. (PCM), I transitioned to the tech field, driven by my love for coding and problem-solving.
